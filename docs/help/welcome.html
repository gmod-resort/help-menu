Welcome

What is this server / gamemode?

This is the Casual Bowling server -- where you can make cash bowling or playing other games and spend it on skins and models. You can even head to the casino to gamble to your heart's content.
Head over to the bowling area to earn some cash!

How Do I Make Money?

There are multiple ways you can make cash on this server.
You can go bowling - which gives you cash according to how well you do.
You can try your hand at the casino - where your luck could bring you good fortune.
The arcade has various games (and will include more soon) that give you cash the higher score you get.
The map provides many ways to make money -- so you're never not making a bit of cash!
You can just stick around and chat - you gain points for just being on the server!

What Can I Spend My Money On?

Primarily you can spend cash on cosmetics such as a new model, new bowling balls or new hats. In the future there may be more ways to spend your hard earning dollar (perhaps even property), so keep an eye out for updates!

Controls

Access your inventory by holding the spawnmenu button (default: [Q]). If you have purchased a skin you can access them here.
Use scroll wheel to go in to third person.
Use [Mouse1] or [E] to interact with 3D UI.
Use spawnmenu button to request videos in the cinema.
Use [G] to browse the taunts and emotes.
