Casual Bowling
January 2019 Changelist

Hey everyone,

We hope you all have had a great holiday season and that your 2019 started off well. With February approaching we've decided to make a changelist with changes during January. As features are added when they are readily available for testing it can be difficult to keep track on what’s been added, removed or modified. Some changes are part due to community feedback - so thank you to anyone who has left valuable feedback that continues to grow the server.

We're planning on adding The Hall of Fame, too. Your 300 score will not be forgotten -- and your face (well, your profile picture) will be forever plastered somewhere on the map sometime soon... we promise.

Condos & Renting

Condos

Added the Luxury Condo

Added the Furniture Store

Added the ability to rent condos and customise furniture (beta phase)

We have added the Luxury Condo as part of an expansion to add player customisable condos and rooms to the server. This will be a testing environment for future condo and renting projects.

You can purchase furniture/equipment, rent out a condo and customise it to your wants and desires -- invite your friends (or strangers) and chill in your space with your rules. Please bear in mind that changes are likely to happen during this testing phase - many things (such as prices and customisation) will be changed.

Gamemodes

Go-Karting

Added Go-Karts

Added the Go-Kart Deathmatch gamemode

Go Kart vehicles have been added to the server and are being worked into a Deathmatch mode and will be available very soon. Some of you have already tested out the new Karts already and they will be worked on for February.

Go Kart Deathmatch will be available in the Game Room. Players will ride their Karts in an open arena (work in progress) and can use Mouse 1 to fire bombs (which use bowling ball trails) to take out enemies. The game's rules will be changed constantly until they feel right - be sure to suggest any rule/gamemode changes through the feedback machines.

Whilst Go-Kart DM is available -- a proper Racing course will be a project for the developers. It's far on the horizon, but is a goal for Karting.

Thanks to BananaMan, Phantasia and Tune Ferret for lending their pretty faces for the screenshots!

Buildings

Værsthus

Added the Pub (Værsthus)

Added the ability to drink alcohol and get drunk

Added the Bartender Job

Værsthus has been constructed and is ready to serve. Includes a communal media player, games of pool and a wide selection of drinks.

Players can now act as a bartender - crafting cocktails and drinks and earn some extra cash in a new job gamemode. When someone orders a drink (and there will always be orders), check the guide to see its ingredients and use the drinks on the counter at the back to craft the drinks. You will rewarded based on your accuracy and if the recipient tips well.

Alcoholic and other drinks can be purchased and made by the bartender which has various effects when consumed. Enjoy a chill atmosphere of music, healthy drinking and community at Værsthus (drink responsibly).
